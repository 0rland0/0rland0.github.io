About

understand.ai is a machine learning startup based in Berlin and Karlsruhe. We apply deep neural networks combined with fine human annotations to build models which help you understand your data better.

He is about to graduate in Computer Science at the KIT (one of Europe's leading universities), where he specialized in machine learning and backend architecture. If he is not drawing on his whiteboard you can find him catching waves in France, Spain or Hawaii.
